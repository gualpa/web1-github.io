A compact but powerful JavaScript library I made that calculates the holidays for the current day or in a date range. Who knew how complicated adding the holiday that occurs on the first Sunday following the first full moon that occurs on or after the vernal equinox, which is the moment when the sun crosses the celestial equator, marking the start of spring in the northern hemisphere, (e.g., Easter!), could be?
